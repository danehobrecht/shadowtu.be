What is shadowbanning?

Shadowbanning, otherwise known as "stealth banning", or, "ghost banning", is the uncommunicated partial or complete removal of content from an online community. The type of effected content varies by service.

What incentivizes a platform to shadowban in the first place?

If content goes against a platform's poltical, social, or financial agenda, it becomes subject to shadowbanning. There are cases of shadowbanning that base on unknown automated standards.

What can be done?

Awareness is key. If enough attention is brought to an instance of shadowbanning, it could threaten the platform in differing ways, to which actions are sometimes reversed. Usually a creator seeks evidence by consulting audiences on alternative platforms. Considering said creator might post similarly challenging content on other platforms with their own secretive banning, this is a problematic approach. Uncompromised evidence is crucial, and this website serves to fill the need; to work towards algorithmic equity.
